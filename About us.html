About Us

Hi, my name is Univexity, and I created this platform with a simple mission: to help game creators protect their work. In the world of online gaming, copycats can appear quickly, and it is incredibly unfair to those who invest countless hours building unique experiences. This board serves as a transparent system where takedown requests can be tracked publicly, giving both visibility and accountability to the process.

Game creation is a labor of love, requiring dedication, creativity, and passion. Unfortunately, when someone copies your game without permission, it diminishes your hard work and can demotivate even the most talented creators. I wanted to create a space that not only reports these incidents but also celebrates the original creators by ensuring they receive proper recognition and protection for their work.

Through this system, you can submit a takedown request for any copied game you encounter, and it will appear publicly for all to see. The transparency of this board is essential; it allows the community to understand what has been reported, preventing repeated violations and encouraging fair play in the gaming ecosystem. By making submissions public, creators, moderators, and even players can all be aware of problematic copies.

Our platform is built with simplicity in mind, but with powerful functionality. Each submission is stored securely, timestamps are tracked, and users can copy game links for easy reference. We also prioritize safety and transparency, ensuring that only genuine reports are submitted. Misuse of this system is strongly discouraged to maintain trust within the community.

Beyond just reporting copies, this project reflects my personal belief in fairness and respect within the gaming world. I want every creator to feel supported and empowered, knowing there is a system that values their contributions.
